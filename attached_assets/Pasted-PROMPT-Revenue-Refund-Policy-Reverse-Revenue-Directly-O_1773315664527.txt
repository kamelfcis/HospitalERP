PROMPT — Revenue Refund Policy (Reverse Revenue Directly)
Objective

Define the accounting policy for refunds issued after OPD consultation revenue has already been recognized, using a direct reversal of the original revenue account (4166) instead of a contra-revenue account.

This policy intentionally keeps the accounting simple and aligned with the existing chart of accounts.

Accounting Policy — Revenue Refunds

For this system iteration, refunds after service delivery must reverse the original revenue directly.

Do NOT introduce a new contra-revenue account such as "Sales Returns".

The system must post the refund against the same revenue account used in the recognition entry.

Revenue account used:

4166 — إيراد كشوفات العيادات الخارجية
Event Definition
Event Type
OPD_REVENUE_REFUND

Triggered when:

appointment.accounting_posted_revenue = TRUE
AND
refundAmount > 0
Journal Entry

If revenue was previously recognized, post the following entry:

Dr 4166            refundAmount
Cr Treasury GL     refundAmount

Explanation:

• Debit reduces previously recognized revenue
• Credit returns cash to the patient

This effectively reverses the original revenue entry.

Validation Rules

Before posting the refund entry:

Ensure revenue has already been recognized:

appointment.accounting_posted_revenue = TRUE

If not:

Throw error:

"لا يمكن رد مبلغ قبل ترحيل إيراد الكشف"

Refund cannot exceed paid amount:

refundAmount <= paid_amount

If violated:

Throw error:

"قيمة الرد أكبر من المبلغ المدفوع"

Refund must include treasury:

Treasury account is mandatory because cash is leaving the hospital.

If treasury_id is missing:

Throw error:

"يجب تحديد الخزنة لعملية رد المبلغ"
Data Recording

When a refund occurs, update appointment row:

refund_amount = refundAmount
refund_reason = provided reason
Idempotency

Prevent duplicate refund postings using:

(source_id, source_entry_type)

Where:

source_entry_type = OPD_REVENUE_REFUND

Only one refund entry is allowed per refund operation.

Audit Logging

Log the operation in accounting_event_log:

Fields:

event_type = OPD_REVENUE_REFUND
status = success / failure
error_message

Every attempt must be logged.

Period Lock Protection

Before posting the entry:

Check fiscal period status.

If closed:

Throw hard Arabic error:

"الفترة المالية مغلقة — لا يمكن ترحيل قيد رد الإيراد"
Important Constraint

Do NOT create or use any of the following:

• Sales Returns account
• Revenue adjustments account
• Contra revenue accounts

All refunds must directly reverse account 4166.

Expected Result

After implementation:

Refunding a consultation that already posted revenue will produce:

Dr 4166
Cr Treasury

Reducing total OPD revenue and returning cash to the patient.
