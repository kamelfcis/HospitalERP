SCENARIO 1 — Night Shift (Midnight Crossing)

Steps:
1) Open shift at 23:00 (simulate if needed via DB timestamp override)
2) At 00:30 next day:
   - load cashier screen
   - fetch pending invoices
   - perform collect on 1 invoice

Expected:
- shift.status = "open"
- isStale = false
- business_date unchanged (same as opening day)
- collect succeeds normally

FAIL if:
- shift becomes stale after midnight
- business_date changes
- collect blocked

---

SCENARIO 2 — Two Active Shifts Same Unit

Steps:
1) Open Shift A (Cashier 1)
2) Open Shift B (Cashier 2) same unit
3) Load cashier screens on both
4) Fetch pending invoices

Expected:
- both shifts see same pending invoices
- no claim occurs on read
- claimed_by_shift_id remains NULL

Then:
5) Cashier 1 collects invoice X

Expected:
- claimed_by_shift_id = Shift A
- Cashier 2 cannot collect same invoice
- receives clear error: "invoice already claimed"

---

SCENARIO 3 — Concurrent Collection (Race Condition)

Steps:
1) Same invoice visible on two cashier sessions
2) Trigger collect at SAME TIME (parallel requests)

Expected:
- only ONE succeeds
- second fails with:
  - clear error message
  - no duplicate receipt
- DB:
  - only one receipt row
  - claimed_by_shift_id set once

FAIL if:
- duplicate collection
- silent overwrite
- both succeed

SCENARIO 4 — Close Shift With Pending Invoices

Case A — single shift:

Steps:
1) Only one shift open
2) invoices pending exist
3) attempt close

Expected:
- close rejected
- clear Arabic error
- no state change

Case B — two shifts:

Steps:
1) Shift A + Shift B open
2) pending invoices exist
3) close Shift A

Expected:
