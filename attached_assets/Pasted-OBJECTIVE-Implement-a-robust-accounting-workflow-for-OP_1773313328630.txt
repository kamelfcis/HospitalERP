OBJECTIVE
Implement a robust accounting workflow for OPD clinic consultations to ensure correct revenue recognition and prevent premature revenue posting. The system must treat clinic consultation payments as **deferred revenue (advance from patient)** until the consultation is completed.

This workflow must be atomic, idempotent, and fully auditable.

---

ACCOUNTING DESIGN

Accounts involved:

Revenue Account
4166 — OPD Consultation Revenue

Deferred Revenue Account
21163 — OPD Consultation Advances (Clinic Booking Advances)

Treasury / Cash Accounts
Example:
12129 — Internal Medicine Clinic Treasury
12130 — Cardiology Clinic Treasury

Treasury accounts are retrieved dynamically from the clinic configuration.

---

EVENT 1 — APPOINTMENT BOOKED AND PAID (CASH)

Trigger condition:

* appointment created
* payment_type = CASH
* payment_status = PAID

Accounting entry must be generated immediately.

Entry:

Dr  Treasury GL Account (clinic treasury)
Cr  21163 OPD Consultation Advances

Example:

Dr 12129 Clinic Treasury
Cr 21163 OPD Consultation Advances

Important rule:
Revenue MUST NOT be recognized at booking time.

---

EVENT 2 — CONSULTATION COMPLETED

Trigger condition:
consultation_status = COMPLETED

System must convert the deferred revenue to real revenue.

Entry:

Dr 21163 OPD Consultation Advances
Cr 4166 OPD Consultation Revenue

Example:

Dr 21163 OPD Consultation Advances
Cr 4166 OPD Consultation Revenue

This event represents actual service delivery and therefore triggers revenue recognition.

---

EVENT 3 — APPOINTMENT CANCELLED AFTER PAYMENT

Trigger condition:
appointment_status = CANCELLED
AND payment already exists

Refund must be issued and accounting entry reversed.

Entry:

Dr 21163 OPD Consultation Advances
Cr Treasury GL Account

Example:

Dr 21163 OPD Consultation Advances
Cr 12129 Clinic Treasury

---

EVENT 4 — PATIENT NO-SHOW

If patient does not attend and no refund is issued:

NO accounting conversion occurs.

Advance remains in:
21163 OPD Consultation Advances

This allows either:

* refund later
* rescheduling
* administrative decision

---

TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

1. Posting must occur inside the same database transaction as the operational event.

2. Accounting entries must be idempotent.

Add unique constraint:

unique(source_type, source_id, entry_type)

Example values:

source_type = CLINIC_APPOINTMENT
source_id = appointment_id
entry_type = OPD_ADVANCE_RECEIPT

and

entry_type = OPD_REVENUE_RECOGNITION

This prevents duplicate journal entries even if the event fires twice.

3. Each appointment must contain:

accounting_posted_advance (boolean)
accounting_posted_revenue (boolean)

These flags prevent repeated posting.

4. Revenue recognition must only happen if:

consultation_status = COMPLETED
AND accounting_posted_revenue = false

5. Refund posting must only occur if:

appointment_status = CANCELLED
AND accounting_posted_advance = true

---

DATA SOURCES

Revenue account:
services.revenue_account_id

Treasury account:
clinics.treasury_id → treasuries.gl_account_id

---

STRICT RULES

1. Appointment booking must NEVER generate revenue directly.

2. Consultation completion is the ONLY event that generates OPD revenue.

3. Insurance or contract appointments do not generate treasury entries at booking.

4. Accounting entries must reference:

source_type
source_id
clinic_id
patient_id
appointment_id

for full audit traceability.

---

EXPECTED RESULT

This implementation guarantees:

* IFRS-compliant revenue recognition
* no premature revenue
* safe handling of cancellations
* prevention of duplicate journal entries
* clean audit trail between medical events and accounting

---

FINAL NOTE

This workflow must be treated as a core financial rule of the system and must not be bypassed or replaced by manual accounting posting.
