PHASE 3 GOAL
==================================================

Close the financial loop of the Contracts module.

PHASE 3 — CLAIM GENERATION
==================================================

Create:
server/lib/contract-claim-generator.ts

Function:
generateClaimsForInvoice(invoiceHeaderId, tx)

Rules:
- use stamped invoice line data only
- claim only lines with companyShareAmount > 0
- skip not_covered
- find or create open draft batch per company/contract/time period
- create claim lines idempotently
- update batch totals

Integrate into patient invoice finalization flow inside transaction.

==================================================
PHASE 3 — ACCOUNTING INTEGRATION
==================================================

Use stamped line split to support company receivable handling.

Required principle:
At billing time:
- patient AR for patientShareAmount
- company AR for companyShareAmount
- revenue according to contractPrice / line pricing result

At settlement:
- settle company AR only
- do not recognize revenue again

At rejection:
- handle explicitly
- do not silently absorb

If a claim line is rejected:
- move liability according to controlled business rule
- keep audit trail
- generate accounting correction path safely
