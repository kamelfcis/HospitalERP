Context:
We discovered a critical accounting bug in sales_invoice journal generation:
- revenue_drugs can resolve to an inventory/warehouse account (12xxx) instead of a revenue account (41xx/42xx)
- this happened because:
  1) account_mappings contained wrong rows at warehouse level
  2) current priority rules allowed warehouse-specific mapping to override the correct pharmacy-specific revenue mapping
- result: journal stays mathematically balanced, but is materially wrong accounting-wise

1) Revenue lines must NEVER post to inventory/stock accounts
